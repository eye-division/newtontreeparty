Tread in the footsteps of Newton …

Posted on 16th June 2014 by Paul Wilson

We’re gearing up for the first weekend of our Newton walks on June 21&22. The meeting points for each day are published below. Remember  if you do not wish to do the entire route, you can meet the group at the half-way meeting points.

Sign up by contacting Ameneh Enayat – ameneh@rosaproductions.co.uk.

Sorry – no dogs allowed!

IN NEWTON’S FOOTSTEPS – WALK 1 – SATURDAY 21st JUNE
The first day’s walk will depart from Grantham and go via Boothby Pagnell, Bassingthorpe, Burton Le Coggles and finishing at Colsterworth.

9.00am Meeting point:  St Wulfram’s Church –
2.00pm Half way meeting point:  Pub Cholomey Arms  – transport option back to Grantham @ 2.30pm
6.00pm Finish Woolsthorpe Manor – return transport to Grantham, dropping off at Burton Le Coggles for those who joined at this point.

IN NEWTON’S FOOTSTEPS – WALK 2 – SUNDAY 22nd JUNE
Setting out from North Witham we’ll be walking up the west side of A1 to Woolsthorpe to Skillington, Stoke Rochford, Grantham

8.15am Transport departs from Angel and Royal, Grantham
9.00am Meeting point: North Witham, Church of St Mary, Church Street
2.30pm Half way meeting point: Stoke Rochford Hall – in the lounge – transport back to Grantham for those attending for half day
5.00pm Angel & Royal, Grantham
