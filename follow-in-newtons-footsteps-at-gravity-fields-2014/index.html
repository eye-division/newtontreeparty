Follow in Newton’s Footsteps at Gravity Fields 2014

Posted on 25th September 2014 by Paul Wilson

 An invitation to join us on foot or online!

Ali Pretty and Richard White have created walks retrace Isaac Newton’s life and family connections in the South Kesteven landscape. The walks are the result of a collaboration with historian Russell Newton, local community members and volunteers from the Lincolnshire’s Age of Scientific Discovery project, supported by the Heritage Lottery Fund.

Join the walks on Friday 26th September & Saturday 27th September

Ali and Richard have also been awarded an Arts Council grant to support the project. Working with local community members,  Ali has created a series of stunning flags that reflect key locations on the Newton Footstep’s route. The flags will be presented on route – come and join in the celebrations!

Richard White will be creating a digital interactive multimedia backdrop to the Newton Walks taking place during the GravityFields Festival on Friday 26th and Saturday 27th September.

You will be able to join the walk at various points or respond through the ether!!

We are using the following platforms:

  • Twitter: please use the @tag @gravityfields in your tweets
    • You can also follow Richard  @walknowlive
  • Flickr: share images to the group here: https://www.flickr.com/photos/gravityfields2014/
  • Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/GravityFields
  • follow Richard and Ali here: www.facebook.com/nowwearewalking
  • Viewranger: http://www.viewranger.com/en-gb
  • Join the community at http://my.viewranger.com/
  • Social Hiking:  http://www.shareyouradventure.com/
  • Please use the tag #newtonwalk in all your social media posting about this project!

Practical Walking Requirements – Please ensure you are suitably dressed, fit enough to walk the route and bring water and refreshments.  Dogs are not allowed. By joining the walk you agree to have your photograph taken along the route as one of the group of walkers.

If possible please bring a smartdevice/phone/tablet.  For full value you will need at least twitter or facebook accounts enabled on the device.

Bring a packed lunch. Pubs for drinks and snacks only.
