Help us get this Newton Tree Party started!

As with all great parties, it’s always appreciated when guests bring something along. Rather than dusting off a bottle of wine, for this party we’re offering you the chance to get really creative, and be part of the very fabric of the Gravityfields festival!

Join together with Ali and Richard – be part of something amazing!

As part of the Newton Tree Party project,  between Monday 8th and Saturday 13th September over at the Grantham Museum our Newton Walk leaders  Ali Pretty and Richard White (pictured above) will be hosting a series of free participatory workshop sessions.

Artistic expert Ali is looking for your input towards creating 6 giant Newton themed flags which will be displayed along the route and later paraded into the festival. She will be residence at the museum for the full week between Monday 8th and Saturday 13th September. Further details will be published shortly:

Digital expert Richard , is looking for your Newton themed digital photos as well as sound recordings which will be brought together to form an interactive multimedia backdrop to the Newton Walks taking place during the GravityFields Festival on Friday 26th and Saturday 27th September.

For 3 days between Thursday 11th and Saturday 13th September Richard will be in residence at the Grantham Museum, providing practical guidance on social media set up, sharing content and also a taster walking session to try out your digital skills.
